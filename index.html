Deal Evaluation Method

This algorithm evaluates deals based on four factors: vouchers sold, sold per day, net revenue, and conversion rate. Each factor is normalized and then weighted according to its importance. The weighted factors are summed to calculate a Deal Score, and the deals are ranked based on their Deal Score in descending order.

Factor Weight
Vouchers Sold 0.25
Sold per Day 0.1
Net Revenue 0.25
Conversion Rate 0.4
